Marvel Studios

It’s staggering to think that 13 years ago, the Marvel Cinematic Universe was yet to assemble many Avengers. Now, 26 films later and 5 TV show later, the series has introduced 27 world-saving characters. After the introduction of the originals – including Iron Man, Captain America and Black Widow – it seemed unlikely that characters introduced in future films would rank alongside them.
